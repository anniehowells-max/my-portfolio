## Challenge

Oetker Collection is one of the world's most respected collections of luxury hotels, known for its heritage of understated elegance and exceptional quality. The brief was to design a digital retail experience for their boutique — a curated shop offering elevated lifestyle products that extend the brand's hospitality into the home.

The challenge was to create an e-commerce platform that felt entirely at home within the Oetker Collection world. Products needed to be presented with the same sense of provenance, craftsmanship and curation that guests experience in the hotels themselves — without the site feeling like a conventional online shop.

||

## Solution

I began with a strategic review of the product assortment and target audience, developing an information architecture designed to support intuitive discovery rather than transactional browsing. The visual direction draws on restrained colour palettes and generous whitespace to create a gallery-like environment that lets product imagery and storytelling take centre stage.

Product pages were designed in an editorial style, connecting each item back to the hotel experiences and craftsmanship behind it. Considered typography and spatial composition reinforce the sense of curation throughout, positioning the boutique as a refined extension of the Oetker Collection rather than simply a retail destination.
